## Key Obligations

   - Actor: grant recipient

2. Maintain expenditure records for audit review.

   - Timing: throughout award period

3. Request prior approval before major budget reallocation.
   - Actor: grant recipient
   - Threshold: above 10% of category allocation
   - Evidence: [p.5 | chunk_026]

1. Funds may not be reallocated above the stated threshold without prior approval.
   - Evidence: [p.5 | chunk_026]
